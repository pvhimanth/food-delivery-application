Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Browse restaurants, select items, and proceed to checkout. Pay online or choose cash on delivery.

What if there's an issue with my order?

Contact our customer support team immediately. We'll resolve the problem as quickly as possible.

How can I track my order?

You'll receive a tracking link via SMS and email. Use this link to track your order in real-time.

Can I change or cancel my order?

You can modify or cancel your order within 5 minutes of placing it. After that, contact customer support for assistance.
